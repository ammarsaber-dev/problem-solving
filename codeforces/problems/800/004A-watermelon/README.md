# 004A - Watermelon

Tags:

## Problem

One hot summer day Pete and his friend Billy decided to buy a watermelon. They chose the biggest and the ripest one, in their opinion. After that the watermelon was weighed, and the scale showed `w` kilos. They wanted to divide the watermelon in such a way that each of the two parts weighs even kilos, and also they shouldn't be forced to cut the watermelon into 3 or more pieces.

Input: `w` (1 ≤ w ≤ 100)

Output: YES if they can divide the watermelon, NO otherwise.
